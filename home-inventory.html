Post-Inventory Considerations

Large Purchases: After significant purchases (such as furniture, appliances, or electronics), remember to update your home inventory report to reflect these new items.

Regular Updates: To keep your records current, we recommend refreshing your home inventory every 2-3 years, especially if you've made significant changes.

Digital Backup: VeriCount offers secure digital backups of your inventory report, ensuring your information is safe and accessible when needed.
